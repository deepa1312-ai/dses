Empowering Women, Nurturing Children
Building a Thriving Future Together


Welcome to Didactic NGO, where we are dedicated to driving sustainable development with a focus on women and children. Our holistic programs empower women through health, economic, educational, and leadership initiatives, while our child-centric efforts ensure comprehensive education, health and nutrition, protection, and talent development. By addressing the unique needs of women and children, we strive to create a more equitable and thriving society where everyone can reach their full potential and contribute to their communities. Join us in making a meaningful impact and fostering a brighter future.

Our Mission

We aspire to instil hope & contribute in upliftment of underprivileged & vulnerable section of society by providing them education, skills & support them in the career opportunities..

Our Vision

Fostering a society wherein women are empowered enough to lead decent life, all children are healthy, happy and enjoy their rights.

Data Insights

Our NGO has made significant strides in improving lives, educating 40,000 children, protecting 30,000 from violence, and informing 12,500 women about their legal rights. Additionally, we've provided nutrition to 10,500 children and improved sanitation for 6,250, ensuring a healthier, safer, and more educated community.

About Us

Founded in 2009, the Didactic Society for Effective Study (DSES) is dedicated to enhancing educational experiences through structured and impactful teaching methods. Our mission is to provide students with comprehensive resources and support . We are committed to the rehabilitation and empowerment of children and women, offering specialized programs that support their personal and academic growth ent where effective learning flourishes.
