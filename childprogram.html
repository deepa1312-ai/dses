Child-Centric Programs

Education and Development Initiatives

Early Childhood Education

Established 2 early childhood education centers providing pre-primary education, nutrition, and health check-ups to 300 children.

After-School Programs

Offered tutoring, homework assistance, and extracurricular activities to 100 plus school-aged children, supporting their academic and personal development.

Health and Nutrition Programs

Health Camps

Conducted regular health camps offering medical check-ups, vaccinations, and dental care to 113 children.

WASH Education

Taught 4000 plus children about personal hygiene, hand washing, and sanitation through interactive workshops, leading to better overall health.

Child Protection and Welfare

Child Rights Education

Educated 1050 children and community members about child rights, protection from abuse, and reporting mechanisms through engaging workshops and campaigns.

Anti-Child Labour Initiatives

Provided support for awareness for child labourers.

Psychosocial Support

Counselling Services

Provided counselling and support to 153 children facing emotional difficulties, helping them overcome their challenges and thrive.

Mentorship Programs

Connected 50 plus children with mentors who offered guidance, support, and positive role models, fostering their personal growth and development.

Life Skills Training

Taught 222 children essential life skills such as communication, problem-solving, and conflict resolution through interactive sessions, preparing them for future challenges.

Extracurricular and Talent Development

Sports and Recreation

Organized sports events and provided access to sports facilities for 258 plus children, promoting health, teamwork, and physical fitness.

Arts and Culture

Offered classes in music, dance, drama, and visual arts to 323 children, nurturing their creativity and cultural awareness.

About Us

Founded in 2009, the Didactic Society for Effective Study (DSES) is dedicated to enhancing educational experiences through structured and impactful teaching methods. Our mission is to provide students with comprehensive resources and support . We are committed to the rehabilitation and empowerment of children and women, offering specialized programs that support their personal and academic growth ent where effective learning flourishes.
