Women's Health and Wellness Initiatives

Health Awareness Programs

Health Awareness

Didactic conducts regular health awareness programs for women, focusing on menstrual hygiene, clean water and sanitation (WASH)

WASH Education

Teaches women about personal hygiene, hand washing, and sanitation through interactive workshops, promoting better overall health.

Economic Empowerment Programs

Vocational Training

Provided vocational training in tailoring, cooking, handicrafts, and digital literacy to enhance employability and entrepreneurship opportunities for women.

Microfinance and SHGs

Facilitated microloans and financial literacy training through Self-Help Groups (SHGs), supporting small business initiatives by women.

Education and Literacy Programs

Adult Literacy Classes

Implemented literacy programs focusing on reading, writing, and basic arithmetic to improve daily lives and self-reliance of women.

Digital Literacy

Conducted computer and internet training sessions to empower women with essential digital skills for modern workforce.

Legal Rights and Advocacy

Legal Literacy Workshops

Educated women about legal rights including inheritance, domestic violence, and workplace rights through workshops.

Legal Aid Services

Provided legal aid and counseling to women facing domestic abuse, workplace harassment, or other legal issues.

Leadership and Capacity Building

Leadership Training

Developed leadership skills among women through intensive training programs for active community participation.

Community Mobilization

Encouraged women to participate in decision-making processes and local governance, enhancing their influence and representation.

About Us

Founded in 2009, the Didactic Society for Effective Study (DSES) is dedicated to enhancing educational experiences through structured and impactful teaching methods. Our mission is to provide students with comprehensive resources and support . We are committed to the rehabilitation and empowerment of children and women, offering specialized programs that support their personal and academic growth ent where effective learning flourishes.
